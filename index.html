Meet our Ninjago Warriors

Discover the world of Spinjitzu and the fearless ninjas who protect Ninjago!

Kai

Element: Fire

Weapon: Sword of Fire

Kai is the hot-headed ninja of fire and the unofficial leader of the team. His bravery is unmatched, but his impulsive nature often leads him into danger.

Jay

Element: Lightning

Weapon: Nunchucks of Lightning

Jay is the energetic ninja of lightning, known for his quick wit and sense of humor. He is a skilled inventor and always ready with a joke.

Lloyd

Element: Energy

Weapon: Sword of Destiny

Lloyd is the green ninja, destined to be the greatest of all. As the leader, he is wise beyond his years and carries the weight of destiny on his shoulders.
